How do we make competency mastery transparent for ourselves and our students?

The Digital Literacy Competency Calculator (DLCC) represents work at the Columbia Center for Teaching and Learning and Columbia University Libraries to clearly represent the connection between digital literacy competencies and the teaching and learning practices that produce them.

View Frameworks View Gallery

Visualize Mastery

This representation format, which can easily be customized by instructors for different teaching contexts, allows learners to see exactly what is involved in mastering a digital skill, how a specific learning experience contributes to their mastery, and what they will be able to do to show mastery.

Our Goals

  • Create an instructional design process to integrate digital skill instruction with intellectual content by aligning with an appropriate standard.
  • Create a clear, customizable representation format to show learners what skills they will master and at what level.
  • Create a community of practice for sharing and iterating on this work with others.

As part of our release of this representation format for wider adaptation, we are also releasing our recommended learning design process.
